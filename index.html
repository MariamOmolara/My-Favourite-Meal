Rice and Stew Sauce

The Nigerian White Rice and Stew lets you eat rice with a variety of sauces: Tomato Stew, Vegetable Sauce, Pepper Soup or Banga Soup. This site would take you through the steps on how to cook white rice with tomato stew sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of white rice
  • 1 tsp salt for the rice
  • 1kg of meat
  • 3 cups of stock
  • 4 cups of blended tomato
  • Chilli pepper to taste
  • 1 paprika
  • 2 medium size onion bulb, chopped
  • 1tsp of curry
  • 1/2tsp of Thyme
  • 1 Garlic clove, crused
  • 1tsp of freshly grated ginger
  • 1 Seasoning cube
  • 1 cup vegetable oil

Method of preparation

  1. Boil the rice with salt until its cooked and dried
  2. Season meat properly to get stock
  3. Fry or grill the cooked meat
  4. Pour the vegetable oil into the pot and place on heat
  5. Throw in the chopped onion when the oil is hot
  6. Pour the blended tomatoes into the pot and allow to cook for about 10 mins
  7. Pour in the stock and cook further
  8. Add the seasoning cube, ginger, crused garlic, curry and thyme
  9. Cook for another 20mins
  10. Add the meat and salt to taste
